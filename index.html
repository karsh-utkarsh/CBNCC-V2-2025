At CBNCC, our mission is to foster a culture of learning, collaboration, and experimentation. Through our flagship events — HACK8ON, DevSpark, TechXplore, and Buildathon — we provide a platform for students to create fearlessly, build intelligently, learn continuously, and win together. We aim to inspire every learner to think beyond boundaries, embrace technology as a tool for change, and grow into innovators who shape the future.

Our vision is to empower students to think critically, collaborate openly, and innovate responsibly — turning their ideas into impactful solutions that address real-world challenges. We believe technology is not just a tool, but a language of expression that can redefine industries, communities, and the future itself. By bridging the gap between technical knowledge and creative thinking, we aim to inspire a generation that doesn’t just adapt to change — but drives it.
